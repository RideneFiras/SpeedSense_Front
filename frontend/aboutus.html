About Us

SpeedSense is an AI-powered tool that helps telecom operators predict 5G throughput, by learning from real-world network and environmental data, it delivers smart, actionable insights to improve Quality of Service before issues arise.

Unlock your business potential

Delivering predictive 5G intelligence to propel your network forward

With SpeedSense, telecom providers can anticipate performance issues before they impact users. Our mission is to simplify Throughout prediction using network and environmental data, helping operators:

  • Anticipate and reduce performance degradation
  • Improve user experience across 5G services
  • Turn technical insights into smarter operational decisions

  • Equip telecom providers with explainable AI tools
  • Unlock the full potential of 5G connectivity
  • Promote sustainable, intelligent network management across all regions

Our strategy blends AI, feature engineering, and SHAP-based explainability into a robust prediction system. We apply:

  • Smart data preprocessing and model pipelines
  • Automated model selection (AutoML + custom tuning)
  • Continuous feedback to improve accuracy and usability

To help operators:

  • Predict throughput behavior
  • Respond before users experience issues
  • Scale smarter, not harder
